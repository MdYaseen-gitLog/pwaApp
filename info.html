Security Notice:

This Progressive Web App (PWA) is installed directly through your browser and does not contain or download any native files (like APKs). Because it runs inside a secure sandboxed environment, mobile security or antivirus apps may not scan it like traditional apps. This is normal and expected behavior.

All data is stored locally in your browser cache and no elevated device permissions are required or requested.
